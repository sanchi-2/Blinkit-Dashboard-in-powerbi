# Blinkit-Dashboard-in-powerbi
The Blinkit Grocery Sales Dataset contains detailed information related to product sales and outlet performance in the quick commerce retail sector. It includes data about various grocery products, their characteristics, and the outlets through which they are sold. The dataset captures important attributes such as item type, fat content, visibility, weight, and pricing, along with outlet-specific details like size, location, and establishment year.
In addition, the dataset includes key performance indicators such as total sales and customer ratings, which help in analyzing product demand and customer satisfaction. By examining this dataset, businesses can identify sales trends, understand how product features influence purchasing behavior, and evaluate the performance of different outlets across regions.
The dataset is used in this project to perform data cleaning, transformation, and analysis using SQL. The insights generated are further used to create interactive dashboards in Power BI, enabling better visualization of sales patterns and business performance. This analysis helps in optimizing inventory management, improving sales strategies, and enhancing customer experience.
Dataset Columns:
Item_Identifier – Unique identifier for each product
• Item_Weight – Weight of the product
• Item_Fat_Content – Indicates whether the product is low fat or regular
• Item_Visibility – Percentage of total display area allocated to the product in stores
• Item_Type – Category or type of the product (e.g., Dairy, Snacks, Beverages)
• Item_MRP – Maximum Retail Price of the product
• Outlet_Identifier – Unique identifier for each outlet
• Outlet_Establishment_Year – Year in which the outlet was established
• Outlet_Size – Size category of the outlet (Small, Medium, Large)
• Outlet_Location_Type – Location tier of the outlet (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)
• Outlet_Type – Type of outlet (e.g., Supermarket Type1, Grocery Store)
• Total_Sales – Total sales generated by the product
• Rating – Customer rating of the product
